Welcome to Preparing for an Interview, in 3 Easy Steps!

Our brand new preparation website allows our visitors to access information about How to Prepare for an interview!

Our Objectives:

  • From memory, learners will be able to describe the steps of preparing for a job interview. Their descriptions will be consistent with the 3 Step Process of Interviewing.

  • Given access to a job posting, learners will be able to identify the most important part of the job posting. The information must match the information identified in the 3 Step Process of Interviewing.

  • Given a blank 3 Step Process of Interviewing, learners will be able to fill in the missing components. Their scores should be at least 70% or higher. The information must match the information identified in the 3 Step Process of Interviewing.
